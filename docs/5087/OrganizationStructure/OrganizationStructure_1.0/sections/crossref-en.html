agentc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: http://ontology.eil.utoronto.ca/5087/Agent/Agent

An Agent may be a Person or Organization. This is captured in the Person and Ontology patterns. An Agent can also perform an Activity. We do not include Software or Mechanical Device (at this time). An Agent can be a member of an organization and hold zero or more posts in an organization.
